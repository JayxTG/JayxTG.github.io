About Me

My research focuses on developing autonomous robotic systems that interact safely and efficiently with humans in dynamic environments. I work on learning-based control for robot manipulation, computer vision and deep learning for perception, and vision-language models for task understanding.

My interests span mechatronic system design including UAVs, unmanned vehicles, and soft robotics, with emphasis on developing novel robotic structures and mechanisms.
